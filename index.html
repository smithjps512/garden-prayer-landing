Founded on Faith, Built with Excellence

Garden Prayer Publishing was founded by James Smith, a technology executive with over 20 years of experience building products for companies like Disney, NASCAR, NFL, Samsung, and Live Nation.

But more importantly, James is a husband, mentor, and believer who knows that the best work comes when we seek guidance first. Every platform, every book, every line of code is built with prayer and a commitment to serving others well.

We believe technology should amplify human capability, not replace human connection. Our work exists to give people their time back — so teachers can teach, entrepreneurs can build, and families can be together.
